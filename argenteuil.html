Shabba' hometown

Shabba is originally from the most dangerous neighborhood France called la zup d Argenteuil. La zup d Argenteuil is a neighborhood inside of the city named itself Argenteuil. 15 minutes away from Paris, Argenteuil is one of the city tourist and rich people do not really know about but try to avoid as much as possible because of its high criminality level.

Shabba' hometown sounds

The environment where Shabba grew up and still reside is noisy because of the street life. Police and fire department are one of the main causes of that noise. Let say it would be like living in New York but just a bit quitter sometimes. The weather conditions are mainly like the one we could find in London. Rainy, cloudy but sometimes a bit a sun light could pass through those clouds.
